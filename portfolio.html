    Project 1: Dictionary Search

    Dictionary search that returns the defintion, the date of the word, and the etymology. There is also 3 options to randomly generate a word with the origins from Old Norse, Chinese/Japanese, and Spanish/Latin. It records your past 5 searches so you can go back to it again faster.
